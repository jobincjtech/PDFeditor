How this works

This tool opens your PDF, shows the page exactly like the original, and places editable zones on top of it. Click a text area to edit. Only edited text is shown in the final export, which avoids overlap. You can also add images, drag them, resize them, and remove selected images.

  • Best for normal text PDFs, letters, forms, and reports.
  • Scanned PDFs or complex tables may not rebuild perfectly.
  • This is not true PDF-to-Word conversion, but it is often easier for quick edits.
